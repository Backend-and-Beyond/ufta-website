Welcome to the Universal Fitness Training Academy Membership Program

UFTA has been at the forefront of fitness education in India, dedicated to fostering excellence and advancing the careers of fitness professionals. Our membership program is designed to extend this commitment, offering a platform for continuous learning, professional development, and networking within a vibrant community of experts and enthusiasts.

Becoming a UFTA member means joining an elite group dedicated to evidence-based practices in sports and exercise science, strength and conditioning, and nutrition.

Our program aims to support your journey by providing access to exclusive content, educational resources, networking events, and much more, all underpinned by UFTA's reputation for quality and innovation in fitness education.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about UFTA membership? We've got answers.

How do I renew my membership?

Renewals can be processed online through your member account or by contacting our membership services team directly. We'll send you a reminder 30 days before your membership expires.

Is there a student discount available?

Yes! We offer a dedicated Student Membership category at a reduced rate specifically designed for those currently enrolled in educational programs related to fitness, sports science, or related fields.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI payments, and bank transfers. For institutional memberships, we can also arrange for invoicing.

How quickly will my application be processed?

Standard applications are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Once approved, you'll receive a welcome email with your membership details and login credentials for the member portal.
